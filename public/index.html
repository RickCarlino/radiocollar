RadioCollar

RadioCollar is an HTML5 app designed to help you share your location with others. It's great for those times when you need to give directions but don't have an address handy.

Want to send your location over email or text? Simply send your location link as "radiocollar.herokuapp.com/p/[name of your waypoint]". Your friends will be forwarded to the map automatically. Example: If your location name was 'flapjacks' you would send http://radiocollar.herokuapp.com/p/flapjacks

Note: Your position isn't private. Use common sense when sharing your location.
